## The Cadre Client Portal

Cadre AI provides a centralized client portal for the organizations we work with. It gives you a single place to see your AI program in one view, rather than tracking progress across scattered documents and tools.

## What the Portal Tracks

Inside the portal, clients can track:

- **Tools** — the AI tools and platforms in use across your organization.
- **Agents** — the AI agents deployed for your teams and the work they handle.
- **Training** — leadership and team training progress and enablement.
- **Results** — the outcomes and impact of your AI initiatives over time.

Bringing these together keeps everyone aligned on what has been built, who is using it, and what value it is delivering.

## For Existing Clients

If you are already working with Cadre AI, the portal is where your engagement lives day to day. If you need access or credentials for yourself or a teammate, reach out to your Cadre contact and we will get you set up.

## Requesting Access

Portal access is provided to active clients. If you are new to Cadre AI and would like access, the first step is to get in touch so we can understand your needs and begin an engagement. You can reach us through the **Talk to an AI Strategist** call to action on our **/contact** page, or by email at **hello@gocadre.ai**. Our team will help you with next steps and portal access from there.
