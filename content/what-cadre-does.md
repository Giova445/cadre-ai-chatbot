## Who We Are

Cadre AI is a San Diego based applied-AI consultancy. We work as an integrated AI team for the companies we serve, providing strategy, implementation, training, and ongoing management under one roof. Our tagline says it plainly: **From AI Confusion to AI Confidence.**

Most organizations feel the pressure to "do something with AI" but lack a clear path from experiments to results. We exist to close that gap, so companies don't simply let AI happen to them.

## Our Mission and Positioning

We believe the AI winners will be the ones with a plan, not the ones with the most tools. Buying another subscription or piloting another chatbot rarely moves the needle on its own. What moves the needle is a prioritized roadmap, the right models and workflows, and a team that stays accountable for adoption.

Rather than hand you a slide deck and walk away, we embed alongside your people. We help you decide where AI creates real leverage, build the automations and agents that deliver it, train your teams to use them, and manage the results over time. That end-to-end model is what turns confusion into confidence.

## The Cadre Way

Our values guide how we show up for clients and each other:

- **Growth Mindset** — we aim to be 1% better every day.
- **Extreme Ownership** — we own the problem, the solution, and the outcome.
- **Team First** — we put the collective result ahead of individual credit.
- **Scrappy** — we move fast, stay resourceful, and find a way to deliver.

Together these values keep us focused on outcomes our clients can feel, not activity for its own sake.
