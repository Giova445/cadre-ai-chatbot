## What the AI Maturity Index Is

The AI Maturity Index is a self-serve diagnostic that scores your company across an eight-pillar framework. It gives leaders an honest, structured read on where the organization stands today and where the biggest gaps and opportunities are. Instead of guessing whether you are "behind" or "ahead," you get a clear picture across the dimensions that actually determine AI success.

## The Eight Pillars

The Index evaluates your organization across eight pillars:

1. **Dedicated AI Team** — whether you have people clearly accountable for driving AI forward.
2. **AI Command Center** — a single, company-wide LLM platform rather than scattered, ad hoc tools.
3. **AI-First Culture Shift** — how deeply AI thinking is embraced across the organization.
4. **AI-Enabled Tech Stack** — API access across core systems such as your CRM and ERP.
5. **AI-Healthy Data Assessment** — the quality of your data, because garbage in means garbage out.
6. **AI Agent Readiness** — readiness to run agents as digital employees, with SOPs and monitoring in place.
7. **Departmental Deep Dives** — how AI opportunity and adoption look within each department.
8. **Three-Year AI Vision** — whether you have a forward-looking plan for where AI takes the business.

Scoring across all eight pillars shows not just an overall level of maturity but exactly which areas to strengthen first.
