## Talk to an AI Strategist

The best way to get started with Cadre AI is to talk to a real strategist about your goals. Whether you are exploring your first use case or ready to build, a short conversation helps us understand your business and point you toward the right next step.

Use the **Talk to an AI Strategist** (also shown as **Talk to an AI Expert**) call to action on our site, which takes you to our **/contact** page. You can also reach us directly by email at **hello@gocadre.ai**.

## What to Expect

A strategy call is a working conversation, not a hard sell. We will ask about your current workflows, where AI might create leverage, and what success would look like for your teams. From there we can recommend a starting point, which is often the free assessment, a focused engagement, or our 45-Day AI Strategy Intensive.

## Start Free Before You Commit

If you would rather explore on your own first, we offer two free entry points:

- **Cadre 360 AI Assessment** — a free, guided assessment that maps your business and surfaces opportunities at zero cost.
- **AI Maturity Index** — a self-serve diagnostic that scores your company across an eight-pillar framework.

Both give you a concrete starting point and make any follow-up conversation far more productive.
