## Industries We Work With

Cadre AI applies AI where it produces measurable business outcomes, and we tailor that work to the realities of each industry. We regularly partner with organizations across:

- **Professional Services** — automating research, drafting, and client delivery workflows.
- **Private Equity** — accelerating diligence, portfolio monitoring, and deal sourcing.
- **Real Estate** — streamlining underwriting, listings, and transaction workflows.
- **Financial Services** — supporting analysis, reporting, and client communication.
- **Mortgage and Lending** — reducing manual work across origination and servicing.
- **Construction** — improving estimating, project coordination, and documentation.
- **Retail and E-commerce** — enhancing merchandising, support, and customer experience.
- **Manufacturing and Logistics** — optimizing planning, operations, and the supply chain.
- **Hospitality** — elevating guest experience and back-of-house efficiency.

Each engagement starts from the workflows and constraints specific to your sector, not a generic template.

## How We Organize Work by Department

Applied AI rarely lives in one corner of a business. We also structure our work by department so that the right teams adopt the right tools:

- **Sales** — prospecting, outreach, and pipeline support.
- **Marketing** — content creation, campaign workflows, and analysis.
- **Customer Success** — support automation, knowledge access, and response quality.
- **Executive Leadership** — decision support and strategic visibility.
- **Finance** — reporting, reconciliation, and forecasting support.
- **Operations** — process automation and workflow orchestration.
- **Technology** — engineering enablement and system integration.
- **Legal** — document review, drafting, and research assistance.

## Outcomes Over Hype

Whatever the industry or department, our focus stays the same: identify where AI can remove friction, build solutions your teams actually use, and measure the impact. We prioritize opportunities with a clear return, then expand from proven wins rather than chasing every trend at once.
