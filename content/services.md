## An Integrated AI Team

Cadre AI offers four core services that work together to take a company from strategy through execution and adoption. You can engage us for a single service or as your end-to-end AI partner.

## AI Strategy

Our AI Strategy service is delivered as a **45-Day Intensive** that takes clients from zero clarity to a prioritized roadmap. In a focused, time-boxed engagement, we map your business, surface the highest-value opportunities, and sequence them into a plan your leadership can act on. You leave with a clear view of where to start, what to expect, and how the pieces connect.

## AI Leadership and Facilitation

AI adoption is as much about people as technology. Our AI Leadership and Facilitation service provides executive and team training backed by behavioral science. We help leaders build fluency, align their teams, and create the conditions for lasting behavior change, so new tools translate into new habits rather than shelfware.

## AI Engineering

Our AI Engineering service covers automation, workflow orchestration, and custom AI agents. We build the systems that put your roadmap into production and connect them to the tools your teams already use.

We right-size every solution. That means knowing when to buy, when to automate, and when to build custom. Often a lightweight tool paired with smart automation beats months of bespoke development, and we will tell you when that is the better call. We map the process before selecting tools, and we hold to a validation culture: done means adopted. We instrument observability into what we ship and run 30, 60, and 90-day check-ins to confirm the solution is being used and delivering value.

## AI Agents

Our AI Agents service builds autonomous workers that handle your most complex tasks. These agents operate as digital teammates, taking on multi-step work with the standard operating procedures and monitoring needed to run reliably in your environment.

## How Onboarding Works: Find. Prepare. Implement.

Engagements follow a simple, repeatable methodology:

- **Find** — identify the opportunities where AI creates the most leverage.
- **Prepare** — get your data, tools, and teams ready for what comes next.
- **Implement** — deploy, adopt, and measure, then expand from proven wins.

This keeps the work grounded in outcomes at every step, from the first assessment to live results.
