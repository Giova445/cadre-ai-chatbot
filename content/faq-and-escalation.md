## Frequently Asked Questions

### What does Cadre AI do?

Cadre AI is a San Diego based applied-AI consultancy that acts as your integrated AI team, providing strategy, implementation, training, and ongoing management. Our tagline is From AI Confusion to AI Confidence.

### How do I get started?

The easiest way is to start with the free **Cadre 360 AI Assessment**, or to talk to a strategist directly. You can also take the self-serve **AI Maturity Index** to see where your organization stands across an eight-pillar framework.

### What services can I engage Cadre for?

We offer four core services: AI Strategy (delivered as a 45-Day Intensive), AI Leadership and Facilitation, AI Engineering, and AI Agents. You can engage us for one service or as your end-to-end AI partner.

### Which AI models do you work with?

We are model-agnostic and help you select the right fit. We work across OpenAI GPT, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, Meta Llama, Mistral, and open-source models.

## How Much Does It Cost?

Cadre AI does not publish pricing. Every engagement is scoped to the specific goals, workflows, and complexity of the client, so there is no one-size-fits-all number. The right way to get pricing is to talk to a strategist, who can understand your needs and put together a scope that fits.

A good, no-cost first step is the free **Cadre 360 AI Assessment**. It helps clarify what you actually need, which makes any conversation about scope and investment far more grounded.

## Talking to a Human

If you would like to speak with a real person on our team, we are easy to reach:

- Use the **Talk to an AI Strategist** call to action, which takes you to our **/contact** page.
- Email us directly at **hello@gocadre.ai**.

Whether you have a detailed question, want to discuss a project, or just are not sure where to begin, a member of the Cadre team is happy to help.

## When to Escalate

Reach out to a strategist or email **hello@gocadre.ai** whenever you need pricing, want a scoped proposal, have a question specific to your business, or are ready to move from exploring to building. For a free starting point, the **Cadre 360 AI Assessment** is always available.
