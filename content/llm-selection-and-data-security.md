## Model-Agnostic by Design

Cadre AI is model-agnostic. We are not tied to any single vendor, so we help clients select and configure the LLM or LLMs that best align with your tech stack and business goals. The right choice depends on your workflows, your data, your budget for compute, and the tradeoffs you care about, and we make that call with you rather than for a vendor.

We work across the leading models, including:

- **OpenAI GPT**
- **Anthropic Claude**
- **Google Gemini**
- **Meta Llama**
- **Mistral**
- Plus a range of open-source models

Because we are neutral on models, we can mix and match where it makes sense and change course as the landscape evolves.

## The AI Command Center

A common risk in growing companies is shadow AI: employees pasting sensitive information into personal ChatGPT or Claude accounts with no oversight. That scatters data across accounts no one controls and creates real exposure.

Our approach is to consolidate that usage into a governed **AI Command Center**, a single, company-wide LLM platform. Bringing AI use into one managed environment gives your organization visibility and control, while still giving employees the capabilities they need to do their work.

## How We Approach Your Data

Within the AI Command Center, we emphasize secure, compliant tools and sound data practices. A core principle is to black-box your data so that it is never used to train other models. Your proprietary information stays yours.

This is how we approach data protection in the work we deliver. We are describing our practices and philosophy here, not claiming any formal certification.

## Governance That Enables, Not Blocks

Good governance should make AI safer to use, not harder. By consolidating tools, setting clear guardrails, and keeping your data walled off from external model training, we help you expand AI adoption with confidence instead of restricting it out of fear.
